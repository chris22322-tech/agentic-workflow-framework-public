# Enablement

Role-specific learning pathways that teach you how to apply the framework to your work. Each pathway is a set of interactive lessons designed for your role — no technical background required for non-engineering tracks.

## Two levels of training

**Generic pathways** (this page) work for any CSM, BA, or engineer at any company. They teach the framework methodology using general examples.

**Personalised training** takes it further — generate training materials tailored to your organisation using your org's roles, systems, and workflows. Walk through the [Personalisation Guide](../learning/personalise/01-why-personalise.html) to learn how, or go straight to the [personalisation tools](../personalise/learn.md).

Start with the generic pathways below. Move to personalised training when you want materials that reference your specific systems and workflows.

## What each pathway covers

Every pathway follows the same structure, tailored to the role:

1. **Introduction** — What the framework is, what changes, what doesn't
2. **Finding your workflows** — Systematic enumeration using the four-angle method
3. **Choosing what to automate** — Structured scoring, not gut feel
4. **Mapping your workflow** — Step-by-step documentation with decision logic
5. **Drawing the automation boundary** — AUTOMATE / Human-in-the-Loop / MANUAL
6. **Completing your scope document** — Error paths, data inventory, constraints
7. **What happens next** — Design, build, evaluate (self-service or with an engineer)
8. **Your action plan** — Concrete next steps with timeframes
