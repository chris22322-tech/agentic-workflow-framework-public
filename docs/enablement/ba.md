# BA Learning Pathway

An 8-lesson interactive learning pathway for Business Analysts. Learn how to identify, scope, and prepare your workflows for AI automation — using the structured analysis skills you already have.

**Duration:** ~3 hours across 8 lessons
**Prerequisites:** None. Just knowledge of your own workflows and the systems you use (Jira, Confluence, etc.).
**What you'll produce:** A complete Stage 1-3 pass for one of your real workflows, ready to hand off to engineering or build yourself in a low-code agent builder.

## Lessons

Click any lesson to open the interactive deck.

| # | Lesson | Duration | What you'll learn |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [What is an Agentic Workflow? (BA Lens)](../learning/ba/01-what-is-agentic-workflow.html) | 20 min | How agents automate process flows, what changes about requirements handoff, what stays the same |
| 2 | [Decompose Your Role](../learning/ba/02-decompose-your-role.html) | 25 min | The four-angle method applied to BA workflows — Jira, Confluence, sprint cycles, stakeholder reporting |
| 3 | [Select Your First Workflow](../learning/ba/03-select-your-first-workflow.html) | 20 min | Structured scoring with BA-specific candidates — not gut feel, not what sounds impressive |
| 4 | [Scope the Workflow](../learning/ba/04-scope-the-workflow.html) | 30 min | Workflow mapping, decision logic capture, and the scope document as your handoff artifact |
| 5 | [Design the Agent](../learning/ba/05-design-the-agent.html) | 25 min | Collaborating with an engineer — what you own (action specs, HIL, error handling) vs what they own |
| 6 | [Build — What BAs Need to Know](../learning/ba/06-build-what-bas-need-to-know.html) | 20 min | What happens during build so you can validate against your scope doc; the low-code self-service option |
| 7 | [Evaluate and Iterate](../learning/ba/07-evaluate-and-iterate.html) | 20 min | Requirements-based testing, diagnosing failures, the iteration cycle |
| 8 | [Your First Agent](../learning/ba/08-your-first-agent.html) | 20 min | Guided exercise: walk through Stages 1-3 with the New Feature Request Intake workflow |

## Recommended pace

| Option | Schedule |
|---|---|
| **Intensive** (half day) | All 8 lessons in one sitting with breaks between lessons 4 and 5 |
| **Spread out** (1 week) | Lessons 1-3 on Day 1, Lessons 4-5 on Day 2, Lessons 6-8 on Day 3 |
| **Self-paced** (2 weeks) | 2 lessons per session, 2 sessions per week |

!!! tip "Start with Lesson 1"
    Even if you're tempted to skip the introduction, Lesson 1 addresses the most important question first: "How does this change the way I write requirements and hand off to engineering?" Get that answered before diving into methodology.
