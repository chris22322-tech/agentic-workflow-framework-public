# Stage 1: Decompose — Organisation Analysis

You are analysing how a specific organisation would apply Stage 1 (Decompose) of
the Agentic Workflow Framework.

## Inputs

1. **Organisation config**: Read `personalise/org-config.yaml`
2. **Stage documentation**: Read `docs/stages/01-decompose.md`

## What to Produce

### Role-by-Role Application

For each role in the org config, describe how they would run the Decompose stage:
- What their workflow inventory would look like
- 5-7 concrete workflows they should inventory (realistic for their role description)
- How to group and categorise their workflows
- Which workflows are candidates for automation vs which are inherently human

### Adaptation Notes

Where the framework's generic Decompose guidance needs adjustment for this
organisation's context:
- Industry-specific workflow patterns
- Regulatory or compliance considerations that affect what can be inventoried
- Client-facing vs internal workflow distinctions

### Systems Landscape

What systems each role likely touches during their workflows. Map data sources
to the systems listed in the org config. Identify gaps — workflows that depend
on systems not listed.

### Recommendations

Specific suggestions for how this organisation should run Stage 1 differently
from the generic guidance. Be concrete — name roles, systems, and workflow types.

## Output

Write a structured markdown report. A team lead at this organisation should be
able to read your output and immediately run Stage 1 with their team.
